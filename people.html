Famous people from Shymkent


Nazima Dzhanibekova

Perhaps the most discussed Kazakh woman in Russia, a participant in the show "Songs on TNT", who signed a contract with the famous BlackStar label two years ago.
Nazima was born and raised in Shymkent. By the way, the singer expresses her love for her native city in her songs.


Aliya Yusupova

The famous gymnast, the absolute champion of Kazakhstan in 2000-2009, the multiple champion of Asia and the most successful gymnast from our country today Aliya Yusupova was also born in Shymkent.


Nurzhamal Usenbayeva

The future opera singer Nurzhamal Usenbayeva was born on June 4, 1959 in Arys (100 kilometers from Shymkent).


Aidai Isayeva

The winner of the beauty contest "Miss Kazakhstan 2013". Aidai represented Kazakhstan at the Miss Universe 2014 contest, which was held in the city of Doral (USA, Florida). The first beauty is proud of her origin and traditional upbringing. One of the leading popular show about love.


Layla Sultankyzy

The director of the children's TV channel "Balapan" of JSC RTRK "Kazakhstan", the author and host of popular programs in the country, is also a native of the southern city.


Linda

Russian singer, performing in electronic and ethnic styles, poetess, artist, surprisingly, was born in Kentau.
